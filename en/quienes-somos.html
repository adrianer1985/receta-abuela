Our Philosophy

The Return to the Essential

A reflection on how immediate convenience has replaced the health of our bodies and the balance with our planet.

My grandmother's legacy

The Grandmother's Recipe project was born in the backyard of my childhood, watching my grandmother collect ivy leaves to wash clothes and calendula flowers to heal our wounds. In her world, nothing was wasted and everything had a purpose dictated by the rhythms of nature. Her hands weathered by the earth not only crafted practical solutions, but held a sacred respect for the balance of life.

"They told us we were moving towards a better life, but often the convenience of the plastic container made us forget the purity of what we put in our bodies."

The modern dilemma: Convenience vs. Well-being

Entering modern urban life, I realized a baffling paradox: so-called "evolution" has brought us to a point where superficial convenience has supplanted true well-being. We have traded long-term physical health and the sustainability of our ecosystem for the instant relief of prefabricated chemical solutions.

We have become accustomed to buying detergents full of artificial fragrances and synthetic endocrine disruptors that remain impregnated in the fibers of our clothes and are absorbed daily by our skin. We use commercial toothpastes loaded with synthetic fluoride and industrial surfactants (such as sodium lauryl sulfate) that damage gums and alter our oral microbiome. Even to clean our kitchen countertops, we spray ammonia or chlorine fumes that directly irritate our lungs and eyes.

A bridge between science and tradition

This website is not a manifesto rejecting technology or a radical call to return to the past. It is a conscious invitation to reflect on the real cost of our daily convenience.

Each recipe shared here has been selected from popular wisdom, but evaluated and adapted to modern life under a rigorous impact analysis. We want to prove with numbers, clean ingredients and clear explanations that it is perfectly viable to care for your body, reduce drastically the toxins you are exposed to and live in ecological harmony without sacrificing your finances. It is time to regain control of our well-being, returning to the essentials hand in hand with science.

About the author

The author and creator of this space is Adrián Enfedaque, grandson of the grandmother whose wisdom inspired this project. Through this portal, Adrián seeks to rescue the recipes of his childhood and combine them with contemporary impact analysis to facilitate access to a conscious, practical and ecological well-being.

If you wish to get in touch to share suggestions, contribute a traditional recipe from your family or resolve doubts, you can write directly to: info@recetadeabuela.com.
